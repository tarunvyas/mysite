Hello, My Name is Tarun.

Tarun Vyas (born 15 October) is an Indian actor and Tv anchor. He is an alumnus of Rajasthan University Drama Department, Jaipur.

Tarun has appeared in critically and commercially successful Hindi films 'Shudh desi romance'. Before acting in film industry, he has apperaed in tv serials like santoshi ma &TV, Collector Saab Zee TV and many others, he began his film career in 2011, with Voice dubbing artist position in bollywood with successful films such as Hum Hai rahi Car k, Pal chin, Kya super cool hai hum, Joker, Raaz 3, Jism2, Wasseypur, Ferrari ki sawaari, Dabang-2, Krish 3, Boss, Yeh jawani hai deewani, Jai Ho. Vyas is mimic voice of Bollywood actor Akshay kumar. Vyas was radio jockey for Fm radio stations like Radio Mirchi,Big Fm,All india Radio.
