Hello, My Name is Tarun.

Tarun Vyas is an Indian actor, TV anchor, and YouTuber who was born on October 15th 1977. He attended the Rajasthan University Drama Department in Jaipur and Mastered with honours.

Tarun has acted in the critically acclaimed and financially successful Hindi Movie 'Shudh desi Romance. He began his film career in 2011, with a voice dubbing artist position in bollywood with successful films such as Hum Hai rahi Car k, Pal chin, Kya super cool hai hum, Joker, Raaz 3, Jism2, Wasseypur, Ferrari ki sawaari, Dabang-2, Krish 3, Boss, Yeh jawani hai deewani, Jai Ho and many others section.Vyas is a Bollywood actor who imitates Akshay Kumar's voice. Vyas has worked as a Radio Presenter for FM stations such as Radio Mirchi, Big Fm, and All India Radio. Vyas is now a youtuber with his Bollywood channels - Biography Video -BV Ka TV channel, Bollywood Talkies for Unknown Facts, and he is a mentor for new talent in voice and act, training people online for free on his Youtube channel - Speech & Act. He also has a few other youtube channels - TV KA TV-Devotional, Chacha hindustani-comedy, Kids Masti - Av ka tv,bv ki mini kitchen for minature food & Vlog, These video links can be found in the website's video section.
